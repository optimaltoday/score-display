Identify the single structural pattern that is most significantly interfering with the greatest number of other addressed patterns. This is the highest-leverage unaddressed pattern in the INSIGHT architecture — the one whose intervention would produce the most widespread system-level improvement. Name it with precision.

Within 30 days

Implement the highest-leverage pattern intervention. At 30 days, assess the cascade: have patterns that were addressed individually but were being undermined by this pattern begun to hold more durably? The system-level improvement — not the individual dimension improvement — is the measure of coherence intervention success.
